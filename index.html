Theta Mart allows customers to create an order on our website to have products delivered to them by one of our drivers from our store in Las Vegas within a 20-mile radius. The Theta mart database records each order and tracks the status from when the orderâ€™s placed to delivery. Theta Mart takes 3,000 orders per year and has 5 drivers currently active. Customers can place Orders for Products that will be delivered by Drivers.

Site Functionality:

    Customers: Can add and update existing customers.

    Drivers: Add, update, retrieve, or delete Drivers.

    Orders: Allows an order to be placed for a Customer, as well as retrieves orders, or updates existing orders.

    OrderProducts: Retrieves products that are a part of various orders. It can also update specific OrderProducts for orders, add new OrderProducts to existing orders, as well as delete OrderProducts.

    Products: Retrieves all products and allows searching functionality for a specific product. Also adds new products, and updates existing products.

    OrderStatuses: Allows retrieval, updates, and insertion of new OrderStatuses.
